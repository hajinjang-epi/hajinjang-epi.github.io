About

Hello! I am Ph.D. candidate in Epidemiologic Science at the University of Michigan, advised by Carrie Karvonen-Gutierrez. I am supported by a KFAS PhD Fellowship for up to 5 years of my PhD program. I obtained my B.A. in Public Health (Health Policy and Management) and M.S. in Public Health Sciences (Concentration: Epidemiology and Risk Management) from Korea University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Hannah Oh.
