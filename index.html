About

I am a PhD candidate in Epidemiology at the University of Pittsburgh (expected 2026) and a graduate student researcher at the Center for Aging and Population Health (CAPH). My work focuses on healthy aging, body composition (obesity, sarcopenia), and fall-related outcomes, with an emphasis on cross-cohort harmonization.

Research Interests

  • Healthy aging, obesity, sarcopenia, and body composition
  • Fall injuries and fractures
  • Cross-cohort harmonization
